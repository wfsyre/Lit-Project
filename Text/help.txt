This is a helpful text file for many of the technical situations you may encounter

1. Opening regular documents:
To open a document, simply type in the name of the document exactly as it is shown

2. Opening encrypted documents:
To open an incrypted document, enter the name of the document, being careful to include only the characters from the standard alphabet.
This does not include any of the following  !@#$%^&*

3. Entering new folders:
When you have completed a section of the game, the password to open the next folder should be clear. But first you must enter the folder name exactly
as it is shown.  After completeing this, you now have access to a wealth of new infomation.
